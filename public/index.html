Welcome to the License Explorer

Choosing an open-source license can be complex. This tool is designed to simplify that process by translating a detailed report into an interactive experience. Use the filters below to find licenses that match your project's needs. You can explore licenses based on their core philosophy: Permissive licenses offer maximum freedom with minimal restrictions. Copyleft licenses require that derivative works are also shared under similar open terms. Public Domain equivalents aim to waive all copyright. Click on any card for a detailed breakdown.
